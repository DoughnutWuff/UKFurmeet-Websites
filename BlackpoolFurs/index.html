BlackpoolFurs is a fun, free meetup for furries in and around the Lancashire area.

We are a small but growing community of digital artists, fursuiters and fursuit makers, fans of anthropomorphic animals, and more!

Come and join us on any of our event dates for furry-fuelled fun!

Travel Information

If you are travelling using Public Transport, the Blackpool Transport app and website can help you find your way!
The main pre-meet venue (Subway) is directly opposite Blackpool North, next door to the large Sainsbury's. This can also be accessed by any bus stopping at Talbot Road.
The main meet can be accessed via a 3 minute walk from Blackpool North, or via the tram, 5 or 7 from Blackpool South. (7 from Starr Gate station only)
The mini meet can be accessed via the tram, 3, 7 and 9. All buses stop at Bispham Village, the Tram will stop at Bispham (and is a short 10 minute walk down Red Bank Road from the venue).
